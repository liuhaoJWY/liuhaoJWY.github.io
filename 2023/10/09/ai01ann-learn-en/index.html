Model :
$$f_{w,b} (x) = wx +b $$
$$x \rightarrow f \rightarrow \widehat{y}$$
Cost function:
$$J_{(w,b)} = \frac{1}{2m} \sum_{i=1}^{m}{(\widehat{y}{(i)} - y{(i)})^2} $$
$$\Downarrow$$
$$J_{(w,b)} = \frac{1}{2m} \sum_{i=1}^{m}{(f_{w,b} (x_{(i)})- y_{(i)})^2} $$

Parameters : $$ w,b $$
Objective: $$ \underset{w,b}{minimize} J_{(w,b)}$$

Grandient Descent :$ \qquad (\alpha ：learning\ rate)$
$$
\begin{cases} w = w -\alpha \frac{\partial}{\partial w} J_{(w,b)} \
b = b- \alpha \frac{\partial}{\partial b} J_{(w,b)} \end{cases}
$$
